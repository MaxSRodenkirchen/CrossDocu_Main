# Design concept for **Exploring Creative Technologies**

### Design problem

The content might **get confusing** to people!
The content also might **get complicated.**
People should understand quickly and get inspired. 
Support exchange!

**That's why it needs**
1. An easy-to-understand overview
2. Intuitive operation
3. A professional yet not overly complicated system
4. Little unique twists 

### Key elements:

- Playful landing page
- Moc-navigation (basically md-files in the sidebar)
- Tag-navigation - pool of content
- CrossDocu interface & view modes
- ~ Search (if needed)

### The landing page

- Gives an overview over the navigation options 
- Gives some first insights into some content
- Gives an impression / feeling of what [[In one sentence - Exploring Creative Technologies|ECT]] is about

### The moc navigation

is a Markdown file tagged as "moc" displayed in the sidebar. 
It follows a certain [[Writing Conventions in CrossDocu|writing convention]] so everything shows in order.

- Focus on the links
- Interaction buttons for switching notes

### The tag navigation

as a pool of content under one joint topic.

- Does not need to have strict rules
- Keep it fun an engaging
- Quick navigation (also semi-randomized)

### General content area

- Main focus of attention!
- Good readability
- Clear labeling of different elements and text-formats
