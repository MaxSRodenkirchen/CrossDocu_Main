# Thoughts on How To Document a Design Experiment

### Inputs

- **Goal of the experiment**: What is the experiment intended to achieve? (e.g., understanding user behavior, testing the effectiveness of a learning application, testing hypotheses)
- **Research question/hypothesis**: What specific question or assumption is being investigated?
- **Context**: Why is the experiment relevant? (e.g., as part of a UX project on plastic pollution or climate change)
