# 102 – TOPs: Working with Images

TOPs are the texture operators in TD. They provide us with options for compositing and adjusting images. But unlike image editing programs, they offer the ability to create content procedurally. This allow for creating impressive effects like the [[Feedback Effect in Touchdesigner|Feedback Effect]] or Blooming.
