# Hybrid Search - The Concept

Hybrid search is a combination from a traditional keyword search and a vector-based semantic search. A keyword search is good in finding exact results but it lacks in finding correlated terms. Semantic search works by calculating relations between words with vectors. The results of both algorithms are put together for the hybrid search.
